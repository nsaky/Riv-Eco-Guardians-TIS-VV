The Prohibitor 24x7 offers an innovative and highly efficient approach to capturing plastic waste in rivers. By intercepting plastic waste in rivers and engaging with local communities, it aims to reduce plastic pollution's environmental impact.
Innovative Solution to Tackle Growing Plastic Waste

Welcome to RivEco-guardians, where innovation meets environmental stewardship in our mission to combat plastic pollution in rivers. At the heart of our commitment is the groundbreaking Prohibitor 24×7 project, a visionary solution born out of our deep concern for the devastating impact of plastic waste on aquatic ecosystems. Picture this: heavy-duty floating booms strategically navigating the currents, resiliently standing against extreme floods, tirelessly capturing plastic while allowing the river to flow freely. Our project doesn't stop there; it's a symphony of sustainability with solar panels powering our autonomous system. What sets us apart is not just the technology but our holistic approach. We engage the people, fostering awareness and responsibility. This isn't just about cleaning rivers; it's about building a sustainable legacy. 

Join us in this eco-guardian journey, where every floating boom is a testament to our dedication to cleaner waterways, healthier ecosystems, and a future where rivers thrive without the burden of plastic pollution. Together, let's be the guardians our rivers deserve.
